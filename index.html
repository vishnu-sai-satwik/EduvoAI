Have Questions?

We have answers. Here is how the Eduvo ecosystem works.

What is Eduvo Forge?+

Eduvo Forge is our live engineering simulator. Instead of watching tutorials, you generate real-world software "tickets" (tasks), write the code in our integrated browser IDE, and submit it for an instant Pull Request review by our AI Senior Developer. Passing reviews earns you XP and builds your rank.

How does CV Matcher Pro work?+

Simply paste a Job Description and upload your PDF resume. Our AI acts like a strict Applicant Tracking System (ATS), parsing your document to find missing skills, generating a match probability score, and giving you specific tips to update your resume before you apply.

How does the AI create my roadmap?+

Our AI acts as an expert career counselor. It analyzes your career goal against a vast database of skills, current courses, and job requirements to build a logical, step-by-step learning path customized to your exact budget and weekly schedule.

Is this website completely free?+

Yes, 100% free for you! We earn a small commission from our partners (like Coursera or Udemy) *only* if you choose to purchase a premium course through one of our roadmap links. This lets us keep the heavy AI processing in the Forge and CV Matcher completely free for students.
